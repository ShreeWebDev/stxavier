Hello everyone,
This is Pooja Sirvi, an Ex-Xavier (Batch 2021-22)
Currently, a CA Finalist and alongside pursuing Second year Bachelor of Commerce from Mulund College of Commerce.
Reflecting on my 13-years journey as a student at St. Xavier's English High School, I can truly say that this institution has given me far more than just an education-It has given me a strong foundation for life. From a shy child stepping into the classroom for the first time to becoming a confident individual ready to face the professional world, this school has shaped me in ways I never imagined.
I still cherish those small yet powerful moments- being encouraged by teachers to speak on stage despite my fear, being appreciated for asking questions and being guided patiently whenever I struggled. Those experiences gradually built my confidence and helped me believe in myself.
What truly makes St. Xavier's special is that it goes far beyond textbooks. The teachers here don’t just teach-they mentor, they believe in you, and they constantly push you to discover your true potential.
“A good school can inspire hope, ignite the imagination, and instill a love of learning” and I feel fortunate to have experienced this every single day.
The focus has always been on understanding and application rather than rote learning. I clearly remember how teachers would go beyond textbooks to ensure we understood the 'why' behind every concept. This approach not only made learning meaningful but also helped me develop a calm and analytical mindset to handle complex situations.
Discipline at St. Xavier's is not imposed-it becomes a way of life. From punctuality to balancing academics with activities, the school naturally instilled in me the ability to manage time and responsibilities effectively. These habits continue to guide me even today.
The exposure to extracurricular activities- be it public speaking, quizzes, elocution, mono-acting, interschool competitions or teamwork based events like science exhibitions and sports, played a vital role in shaping my personality. I learned that success is not just about winning, but about learning, improving and having the courage to keep trying.
Today, as I step into corporate world and take on new challenges, I carry a part of St. Xavier's with me in everything I do- the confidence to express myself, the discipline to stay consistent and the mindset to keep growing.
For parents seeking a school that offers more than just academic excellence, I can say this wholeheartedly- St. Xavier's English High School is a place where children don’t just study, they truly evolve. It nurtures character, discipline, builds confidence and prepare students to face the world with strength and integrity.
I will always be grateful for the values, memories, and guidance I received here.
“Roots grow deep where values are strong” and I am proud to say that my roots were built here. I take immense pride in calling myself an Ex-Xavier.
